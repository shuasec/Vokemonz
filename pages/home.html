Upcoming VokemonS Pro Circuit

VokemonS Pro Circuit 2023

The VokémonS Pro Circuit is a competitive gaming circuit that focuses on organized VokémonS video game
and trading card game (TCG) tournaments. It provides a platform for VokémonS enthusiasts to compete at
a high level and showcase their skills in both video game battles and TCG matches. The circuit features
various events and tournaments throughout the year, allowing players to earn points and qualify for
prestigious championships.
